Opt-in Policy — Lokalweb RCS Messaging

Last updated: March 2026

This page describes how Lokalweb collects consent and manages messaging for its RCS (Rich Communication Services) business messages sent to professionals in France.

1. Who receives our messages

Lokalweb sends RCS messages exclusively to French tradespeople and artisans (plumbers, electricians, roofers, painters, etc.) identified through publicly available government registers:

  • SIRET / SIRENE registry — France's official business identification database, publicly accessible
  • RNE (Registre National des Entreprises) — the national business register managed by INPI
  • ADEME RGE directory — public list of certified energy-efficiency professionals

All data used for outreach is 100% publicly available. No private or purchased contact lists are used.

2. How opt-in works

Lokalweb operates an outbound B2B prospecting model. There is no sign-up form or prior registration required. Here is how it works:

  • We identify eligible artisans from public government data sources (SIRET, RNE, ADEME RGE)
  • The first message is the first contact — it contains a personalized website preview created specifically for the recipient's business, along with pricing and a clear option to opt out
  • Every initial message clearly includes instructions to reply STOP to immediately opt out
  • By not replying STOP, users implicitly accept to receive follow-up messages
No sign-up form is required. This is a B2B outbound model based entirely on publicly available professional data. Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

3. Message frequency

Lokalweb sends a maximum of 3 messages per prospect:

  • Message 1: Initial contact — personalized site preview + pricing + opt-out instructions
  • Message 2: First follow-up (only if no response and no opt-out)
  • Message 3: Final follow-up (only if no response and no opt-out)

No further messages are sent after the third contact, regardless of response.

4. Message content

Each message sent by Lokalweb contains:

  • A link to a personalized website preview created for the recipient's business
  • Clear pricing information for Lokalweb's web design service
  • A call-to-action to view the preview or get in touch
  • Opt-out instructions (reply STOP)

Messages are professional, relevant, and directly related to the recipient's trade activity.

5. Opt-out

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message received from Lokalweb. Upon receiving a STOP request:

  • The phone number is immediately added to our blacklist
  • No further messages will ever be sent to that number
  • The opt-out is processed automatically and instantly
To stop receiving messages, simply reply STOP to any Lokalweb message. Your number will be permanently removed.
