Opt-in Policy

Lokalweb RCS Messaging — Last updated: March 2026

Who receives our messages

Lokalweb sends RCS messages exclusively to registered French tradespeople (plumbers, electricians, painters, roofers, etc.) identified through public government databases:

  • SIRET / RNE (French national business registry)
  • ADEME RGE (certified eco-trade professionals)
  • Public professional directories

Recipients are independent business owners or sole traders. No private individuals are contacted.

How opt-in works

Lokalweb uses an outbound B2B prospecting model. There is no prior subscription or sign-up form. The process is as follows:

  • A prospect is identified via public data (trade, city, phone number from SIRET registry)
  • A single personalized RCS message is sent, containing a preview link of a website generated for their business
  • The message clearly identifies Lokalweb as the sender and includes pricing information (25€/month)
  • Every message includes an explicit opt-out instruction: reply STOP to unsubscribe immediately
By not replying STOP, recipients implicitly accept to receive up to 2 follow-up messages. No further messages are sent after opt-out or after the 3-message limit.

Message frequency

Maximum 3 messages per prospect: 1 initial message + 2 follow-ups at most. Once a prospect converts to a client or opts out, all messaging stops permanently.

Message content

Each RCS message contains:

  • A personalized preview link of the website generated for the recipient's business
  • Pricing information (25€/month, no commitment)
  • A clear call-to-action
  • Opt-out instructions (reply STOP)
  • Sender identification: Lokalweb / contact@lokalweb.fr

Opt-out

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Upon receiving a STOP reply:

  • The number is immediately and permanently blacklisted
  • No further messages are sent
  • The opt-out is irreversible unless the user explicitly requests reactivation

For any questions or opt-out requests by email: contact@lokalweb.fr
